About FlashLearn

FlashLearn is a web-based flashcard and quiz application designed to make revision engaging and effective

What We Do

FlashLearn helps students and learners of all kinds revise through interactive flashcards and quizzes. The app encourages active recall over passive reading - helping you retain information more effectively.

Choose from a range of themed decks or create your own decks tailored to your studies
